Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy applies to all apps published by Sandeepak Madhavan on the Freshworks Marketplace. It explains what data these apps access, how it's used, and how it's protected. Each app's specific data access is listed separately in the "Per-app data access" section below.

What is not collected, across all apps

  • No calendar, ticket, or Freshdesk data is sent to, sold to, or shared with any third party.
  • No data is used for advertising or analytics profiling.
  • No data is stored beyond what's needed to operate each app's specific automation, as described per app below.

Where data is stored

Configuration for each app (such as connected accounts or preferences) is stored using Freshworks' own app storage (KVS), scoped to your Freshdesk account and agent. It is not copied to any separate external database or server operated by the developer.

Data retention

Stored configuration is retained for as long as an app remains installed and connected. Disconnecting an integration or uninstalling an app removes its associated stored configuration.

Your choices

  • You control which accounts or integrations are connected to each app, and can disconnect or replace them at any time from within the app.
  • You can request removal of any retained configuration by contacting support (see the Support page).

Per-app data access

The section below lists exactly what each published app accesses. As new apps are published, a new subsection is added here — the rest of this policy applies to all of them equally.

CalendarGuard

  • Google Calendar (read-only): event titles and time ranges, from the calendar URL you provide, solely to detect out-of-office events.
  • Freshdesk: reads and updates your agent availability status, and reads your agent name and email to display them within the app.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as apps evolve. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed via the Support page.
