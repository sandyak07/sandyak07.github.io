Typical response time: within 2 business days.

Before you reach out

To help resolve your issue faster, include:

  • The app name and version
  • What you expected to happen, and what happened instead
  • A screenshot, if the issue is visual (please mask any confidential ticket or customer data)
  • Roughly when the issue occurred

Common topics

  • Calendar not syncing: confirm the calendar URL is still valid and hasn't been revoked, then use "Sync Now" inside the app.
  • Status not changing automatically: check the Upcoming OOO list inside the app for a "Failed" status, which usually indicates a temporary sync issue.
  • Privacy or data questions: see the Privacy Policy.
