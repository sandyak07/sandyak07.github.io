CalendarGuard

In review

Keeps your Freshdesk availability aligned with Google Calendar out-of-office events — automatically, with no public calendar sharing required.

How it works

Connect a Google Calendar (public or private "secret" address), and CalendarGuard watches for events tagged with your chosen out-of-office keywords. When one starts, your Freshdesk status switches to Unavailable automatically — no manual toggling, no forgotten status changes after a meeting runs long.

Features

  • Custom OOO tags — define your own keywords (e.g. "Leave", "Vacation"), not just a fixed "[OOO]" format.
  • Private calendar support — works with Google's secret iCal address, no need to make your calendar public.
  • Hourly automatic sync — plus a manual "Sync Now" whenever you need an immediate check.
  • Skip any event — one click to exclude a specific calendar entry from triggering a status change.
  • Clear status visibility — see at a glance whether you're currently OOO, what's coming up, and why anything failed to sync.
